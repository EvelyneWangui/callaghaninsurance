Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about insurance coverage and our services.

How do I get a quote? +

Getting a quote is easy! You can call our office, visit us in person, or request a quote online through our contact form. We'll ask you some basic information about what you'd like to insure and provide you with customized options.

What payment options do you offer? +

We offer flexible payment options including monthly, quarterly, semi-annual, and annual payments. You can pay by check, credit card, or set up automatic bank payments for convenience.

How do I file a claim? +

You can file a claim 24/7 by calling our claims hotline at 1-800-CLAIM-NOW. For non-emergency claims, you can also file through our website or visit our office. We'll assign you a dedicated claims representative to guide you through the process.

Can I bundle multiple policies? +

Yes! We offer significant discounts when you bundle multiple policies like home and auto insurance. Bundling can save you up to 25% on your premiums while simplifying your insurance management.

How often should I review my coverage? +

We recommend reviewing your coverage annually or whenever you experience a major life change (marriage, new home, new driver, etc.). Our agents will proactively reach out to ensure your coverage remains appropriate for your current situation.
